About Us

The haven for quality data science services!

Hi! my name is Richard, a research data scientist at the Chair of Statistics and Data Science HSU Hamburg. I deploy advanced statistical modelling approaches to proffer solutions to problems frequently encountered with the use of some particular class of models in empirical studies (see Publications).

The DataXotic platform has been helping individuals and businesses with data analysis for over 5 years — providing detailed (and easy to understand!) solutions to various data analytic problems. We have hands-on experience in implementing machine learning algorithms, advanced data science procedures and many more. We ensure to deliver neat and insightful results in every project (see projects), as well as passionate about working with individuals, teams and organizations that would want to make effective use of their data. You are welcome to contact us to discuss both short and longer-term projects. We look forward to being of service to you. Your satisfaction is our satisfaction!
